# MOA
- **Toxicological Effects**: Aflatoxins have various toxicological effects with mechanisms not yet fully elucidated.
- **Regulatory Purposes**: Advanced knowledge serves as a scientific tool for food safety authorities.
- **Mutagenic Effects of AFB1**: Focus of most studies since the discovery, primarily due to the intermediate metabolite AFB1-exo-8,9 epoxide (AFBO).
- **AFBO**: A highly unstable molecule that reacts with cellular macromolecules (nucleic acids, proteins, phospholipids), causing genetic, metabolic, signalling, and cell structure disruptions.
- **Oxidative Stress (OS)**: Increasing evidence shows AFB1's dramatic or higher effects on cell function and integrity through the induction of OS.
